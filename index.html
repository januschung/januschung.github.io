    Hello, I'm Janus Chung

    I'm a Software Engineer with over 20 years of experience across DevOps, Developer, QA and Support.
    I’ve recently joined Flagler Health Flagler Health empowers providers by leveraging the power of AI to help provide clinical and back-office support where I’m leading DevOps efforts and helping scale the infrastructure from the ground up.
    Previously, at Ad Hoc. a digital services company that helps the federal government better serve people I worked on standing up Kubernetes (EKS) and other AWS infrastructure for the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs applications, and collaborated with our government partners to implement GitHub Actions, Argo CD, and Argo Rollouts for continuous delivery and progressive deployment workflows.
    Before that, I spent over seven years with Integral Ad Sciences, a global leader in digital media quality. IAS makes every impression count, ensuring that ads are viewable by real people, in safe and suitable environments, activating contextual targeting, and driving supply path optimization. leading efforts to migrate monoliths to AWS Cloud and modernize CI/CD pipeline.
